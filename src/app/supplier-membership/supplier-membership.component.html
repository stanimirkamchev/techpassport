Choose the membership which is best for you

Our mission is to speed up the process of negotiating POCs with financial services company. We want to make it quicker and easier for the start-up community to connect with large corporate banks.

To that end, we have made our Core plan free to sign up to with no ongoing subscription fees.

We will only charge a fee once you have successfully signed a contract with a buyer following initial engagement on the platform.

The core subscription allows you access to the main features of the platform, and you can mark your products as eligible for Rapid POCs to let buyers know you’re ready to engage.

We also have a Premium subscription with some extra features to help you stand out from other suppliers listing their products on the marketplace.
Premium subscription will be free until October 2021.

If you have any questions about which plan suits your needs best please get in touch with Teresa@techpassport.info.
